St. Gilgen is a small, beautiful and charming village by the lake. There are many more hotels and B&Bs, and most are within walking distance of the wedding location and church.

St. Gilgen can be reached by train or car from Vienna and Munich. There are also direct flights to Salzburg. From Salzburg, it is approximately 40 minutes by car.
